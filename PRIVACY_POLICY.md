# Privacy Policy for Personal AI Employee (Meta Integration)

**Effective Date**: April 24, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how the "Personal AI Employee" application (the "App") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our services, specifically concerning our integration with Meta Platforms, Inc. (Facebook and Instagram).

## 2. Data We Collect
Our App uses the Meta Graph API to perform automated tasks on your behalf. We may access the following information:
*   **Facebook Page Data**: Post content, comments, and engagement metrics for Pages you manage.
*   **Instagram Business Data**: Media, comments, and insights for Instagram Business/Creator accounts linked to your Facebook Pages.
*   **Authentication Tokens**: We store OAuth access tokens locally in your secure project directory (`/credentials/`) to maintain connectivity.

## 3. How We Use Data
The data collected is used strictly for the following purposes:
*   **Content Management**: Scheduling and publishing posts to Facebook and Instagram.
*   **Engagement Monitoring**: Detecting mentions and comments to create "Needs Action" tasks in your local vault.
*   **Analytics**: Generating "CEO Weekly Briefings" to summarize social media performance.

## 4. Data Storage and Security
*   **Local Storage**: All data fetched from Meta is stored locally within your "AI Employee Vault." We do not host your data on external servers.
*   **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. 
*   **Encryption**: We recommend using full-disk encryption on the machine hosting the vault to protect local credential files.

## 5. User Control and Data Deletion
*   **Revoking Access**: You can revoke the App's access at any time via your Facebook App Settings.
*   **Data Deletion**: Deleting the local `/credentials/` folder and the vault content will remove all data retrieved by the App. You may request a formal data deletion by following the instructions in the Meta Developer portal associated with this App.

## 6. Compliance with Meta Policies
This App complies with the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We only request permissions necessary for the "Social Media Management" and "Insights" use cases.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.
